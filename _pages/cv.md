## Brief academic profile

I am a CNRS postdoctoral researcher at Collège de France, working in theoretical condensed matter physics, quantum information, and non-equilibrium many-body systems. I received my PhD in Physics from the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru.

My research focuses on quantum chaos, entanglement, open quantum systems, non-Hermitian dynamics, measurement-induced dynamics, Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev models, quantum glassy systems, and quantum resource theory.
