In this work, we investigate many-body chaos and information scrambling in the quantum spherical \(p\)-spin glass model. We compute the Lyapunov exponent and study how chaos evolves across the quantum-to-classical crossover.

We find that quantum fluctuations can have a nontrivial effect on many-body chaos, including a nonmonotonic dependence of the Lyapunov exponent near the dynamical glass transition. This work connects quantum chaos, glassy relaxation, and scrambling in strongly interacting disordered systems.
